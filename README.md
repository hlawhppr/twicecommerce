## Hidden Costs and Fine Print

Let's talk about what they DON'T shout about on the pricing page.

**Transaction Fees:**
These payment processing fees add up. If you're doing $10K/month in sales on the Discover plan, that's $340 in fees (using TWICE Payments at 3.4%). On the Build plan, same sales would cost you $240. 

Do the mathâ€”sometimes upgrading to a more expensive plan actually saves you money if your transaction volume is high enough.

**What's NOT Included:**
- Additional store locations cost extra (only 1 included on Discover/Build, up to 5 on Advance)
- If you need more than 22 languages... actually, they support 22 languages max
- PCI compliance audit costs (though TWICE itself is Level 1 PCI DSS compliant)

**The Stripe vs. TWICE Payments Decision:**
TWICE Payments is only available in Europe. Outside Europe, you'll use Stripe, which has its own fees on top of TWICE's commission. The commission structure is actually better than it looks because manual payment options have ZERO processing feesâ€”you just handle the payment yourself.
